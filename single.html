NOVA TERRA

A great big worldbuilding project

July 21, 2026 Ryan Lin

I started Nova Terra a long way back. So far that I can't even pinpoint its beginnings. I think, however, it started in about September 2024, when I and some of my friends elected to make a NationStates region in Junior Robotics Club. Although our time there was short-lived, Nova Terra has continued to be worked on ever since then, accruing ever more contributors and ever more useless economic statistics. Now, normally, you'd do this for the sake of having a setting for a novel or something. We at the Nova Terra Club, however, don't. We do this for the love of the excessively detailed lore for 61+ countries. It will likely never be finished, and that's the point.

Right now, we are also taking recruits. Actually, we're always taking recruits! Friendship is a neat thing, isn't it? So contact me if you have questions about joining.
